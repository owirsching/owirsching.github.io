About Me

Hi and thanks for visiting my site! I am a first year at Bowdoin College in Brunswick, Maine. At Bowdoin I play on the women's golf team and create content for the Bowdoin Orient's (student newspaper) social media. When I am not in class, I like to spend my free time learning new skills (this winter break I took a Codecademy course on HTML, CSS, Git, and Github), golfing, and cooking.

I grew up in Blue Bell, Pennsylvania and went to high school at Mount Saint Joseph Academy. There, I played on the golf team and as captian my senior year, led my team to the State Championship title. The Campanile, my school newspaper, was another important part of my high school career and as Editor-in-Chief I helped the publication transition online at the beginning of the pandemic. I was additionally involved with the charity Operation Smile, of which I was a member of the Regional Leadership Committee covering Pennsylvania. For academics, I was recognized as a National Merit Scholar senior year.
